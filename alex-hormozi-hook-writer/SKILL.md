# Alex Hormozi Hook Writer

Write 10 high-converting hooks for any product or content using Alex Hormozi's proven hook framework from $100M Leads.

## How to Use

Tell me about your product, service, or content topic and I will generate 10 powerful hooks ready to use for TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts, ads, or emails.

**Just say:** "Write hooks for [your product/topic]"

## What You Get

10 hooks across Hormozi's core hook types:
- **The Curiosity Hook** - makes people need to know the answer
- **The Contrarian Hook** - challenges what people believe
- **The Result Hook** - leads with the outcome
- **The Warning Hook** - fear of missing out or making a mistake
- **The Story Hook** - opens a loop with a story
- **The Bold Claim Hook** - makes a surprising statement
- **The How-To Hook** - specific, tangible promise
- **The List Hook** - X things you didn't know about...
- **The Question Hook** - direct question that demands engagement
- **The Identity Hook** - speaks to who the person is

## Hormozi's Hook Formula

Every great hook does ONE of these:
1. **Calls out the target audience** ("If you sell X...")
2. **States the dream outcome** ("How I made $X in Y days")
3. **Creates curiosity** ("The reason most people fail at X...")
4. **Challenges a belief** ("Stop doing X - here's why")

Hooks should be 1-2 sentences max. Front-load the value. No fluff.

## Output Format

For each hook, provide:
- The hook text (ready to copy-paste)
- The hook type (e.g., Curiosity, Contrarian)
- Best platform to use it on (TikTok, email subject, ad headline, etc.)
