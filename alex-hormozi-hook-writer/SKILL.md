## The Hormozi Hook Framework

### What Is a Hook?
According to Hormozi, a hook is **the first thing someone sees, hears, or reads** - and it has one job: make them stop and pay attention. In a world of infinite content, the hook is the tax you pay to be heard.

Hormozi's rule: **A great hook does not explain. It creates an unresolved tension that the brain needs to resolve.**

### The Anatomy of a Hormozi Hook

Every effective hook has up to 3 components:

**1. The Call-Out (Who is this for?)**
Explicitly name the person you are targeting. Be specific - vague hooks get ignored.
- "If you're a coach charging less than $5K..."
- "Gym owners with under 200 members..."
- "Anyone who's tried to lose weight and failed..."

**2. The Tension Mechanism (Why should they care?)**
Create a gap between where they are and where they want to be - OR reveal a threat they didn't know existed. Hormozi identifies these tension types:
- **Curiosity:** They don't know something they feel they should
- **Fear:** They might be losing something, making a mistake, or being left behind
- **Desire:** Something they want is closer than they think
- **Contrarian:** Something they believe is wrong
- **Proof:** A result so specific it demands explanation

**3. The Payoff Promise (What will they get?)**
Not always present - but when used, it makes the hook irresistible. Must be specific.
- NOT: "How to grow your business"
- YES: "How I went from $0 to $1.2M in 14 months without running a single ad"

### Hormozi's 4 Hook Formulas

**Formula 1: The Specific Result Hook**
Structure: [Specific outcome] + [Timeframe] + [Mechanism or method]
Example: "How I added $300K ARR in 90 days by changing one sentence on our website"

**Formula 2: The Contrarian Hook**
Structure: [Common belief] + [Challenge] + [Implication]
Example: "Stop A/B testing your landing pages. Here's why it's hurting your conversions."

**Formula 3: The Curiosity Gap Hook**
Structure: [The thing they want] + [The unexpected obstacle or insight]
Example: "The reason most coaches never hit $10K/month isn't their offer. It's this."

**Formula 4: The Audience Call-Out Hook**
Structure: [Target identity] + [Relatable situation] + [Promise]
Example: "If you sell services and hate sales calls - this changes everything."

### What Makes Hormozi Hooks Different

- **Specificity kills skepticism.** Vague claims are dismissed. Specific numbers and names create credibility by being verifiable.
- **Name the enemy.** Every strong hook has something to overcome - a bad habit, a competitor, a false belief, wasted time.
- **Short is not a rule, but clarity is.** Hormozi's hooks range from 5 words to 3 sentences. Length doesn't matter. Unresolved tension does.
- **Hooks work across all formats:** TikTok openers, email subject lines, ad headlines, podcast intros, sales page H1s, Instagram captions.
