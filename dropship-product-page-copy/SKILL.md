## The Framework: The 7-Section Dropship Page

High-converting dropship product pages follow a predictable structure. Each section has ONE job. Skip one section and conversions drop.

### Section 1: Headline (The Grab)

**Formula:** `[Specific Result] Without [Common Pain/Sacrifice] â€” [Timeframe or Qualifier]`

The headline must answer one question in under 5 seconds: "Is this for me?"

**Examples:**
- "Finally Sleep Through the Night Without Drugs or Gimmicks"
- "Fix Your Posture in 14 Days Without Expensive Chiropractor Visits"
- "Lose the Back Pain That's Ruined Your Last 10 Flights"

**What NOT to do:** Generic product names ("LED Strip Lights"), clever wordplay nobody gets, or question headlines that don't promise anything.

**Formula:**
1. Mirror the problem in their words
2. Name the frustration/failure they've had before
3. Hint that there's a better way (without revealing it yet)

**Template:**
```

You've probably even [second attempt they made].

The truth is, [real cause of problem] â€” and that's exactly what [product name] was built to fix.
```
