# Rich Schefren Strategic Profits System

## What This Skill Does

This skill applies Rich Schefren's Strategic Profits frameworks to help you stop being an "opportunity seeker" and start running your business like a true entrepreneur. Schefren is known as the "guru's guru" - he personally coached Frank Kern, John Reese, Mike Filsaime, Jeff Walker, and dozens of other 8-figure marketers before they hit their biggest breakthroughs.

His most famous work: the **Internet Business Manifesto** (2006), downloaded 100,000+ times in its first week, and the **Attention Age Doctrine** - both changed how online marketers think about building real businesses.

**Use this skill when you need to:**
- Escape the "opportunity addict" trap and build systems instead of chasing tactics
- Write marketing that positions you as a "Strategic Advisor" instead of a vendor
- Build a Lead Conversion Machine (LCM) that nurtures prospects automatically
- Create "continuity income" that doesn't depend on constant launches
- Write content that positions you as the go-to authority in your niche
- Scale without working more hours

## The Core Schefren Problem: Why Most Marketers Stay Stuck

Schefren's famous diagnosis: **Most online business owners are "opportunists" not "entrepreneurs."**

The difference:
- **Opportunist** - chases the next tactic, jumps from thing to thing, their income stops when they stop working
- **Entrepreneur** - builds systems, creates leverage, their business works without them

Schefren's "3 Pillars of Entrepreneurship":
1. **Systems** - Documented, repeatable processes that run without you
2. **Strategy** - A long-term plan, not just today's tactic
3. **Leverage** - Using other people's time, money, audiences, and credibility

Before writing any copy or building any funnel, ask: **"Am I building a business or creating a job for myself?"**

## FRAMEWORK 1: The Attention Age Doctrine

Schefren's key insight: **Attention is the scarcest resource online.** Your biggest competitor isn't another product - it's everything competing for your prospect's time and focus.

**The Attention Audit (apply to any marketing message):**

```
1. STOP: Does this grab attention in the first 3 seconds?
   - Does the headline stop the scroll?
   - Is there a specific, surprising claim or curiosity gap?

2. HOLD: Does this maintain interest through the body?
   - Is there a clear "promise of progress" every paragraph?
   - Are there pattern interrupts (questions, bold text, short paragraphs)?

3. DIRECT: Does this tell them exactly what to do next?
   - Is there ONE clear next action?
   - Is the next action low-friction (click, watch, download)?

4. REWARD: Does this deliver on the promise immediately?
   - Does the thing they clicked on match what they expected?
   - Is the first 30 seconds of your video/page worth staying for?
```

**Schefren Attention Positioning Formula:**
> "I help [specific audience] [achieve specific result] by [unique mechanism] - without [biggest frustration]"

Example: "I help Shopify store owners get their first 100 sales by setting up a 3-email abandoned cart sequence - without running ads or hiring a developer"

## FRAMEWORK 2: The Lead Conversion Machine (LCM)

Schefren's system for turning cold leads into buyers automatically. The LCM is not a sales funnel - it's a relationship-building machine that positions you as the trusted authority before any pitch.

**The 5 Stages of the LCM:**

### Stage 1: Positioning Content (Authority Hook)
- A piece of content that immediately establishes WHY you're the expert
- NOT promotional - purely educational and valuable
- Format: Video, report, or article that reveals a "contrarian insight" in your market
- Goal: Make prospects think "I've never heard anyone explain this so clearly"

Template:
```
"Most [audience] believe [common belief]. Here's why that's keeping them stuck,
and the [contrarian approach] that [specific successful people] actually use..."
```

### Stage 2: The Core Belief Shift
- Content that challenges ONE core belief keeping them from buying
- Not "why our product is great" - it's "why your current approach will never work"

Template:
```
Subject: The real reason [audience] struggle with [problem]

Everyone tells you [common advice]. But here's the dirty secret: [why that advice fails].

The people actually getting [result] aren't doing [common approach]. They're doing [your mechanism].

Let me show you exactly what I mean...
```

### Stage 3: The Proof Bank
- Systematically collect and present evidence that your mechanism works
- Structure: "Here's how [person like your prospect] went from [before state] to [after state] using [your method]"

### Stage 4: The Soft Offer (Tripwire)
- Small paid offer that converts buyers from non-buyers
- Price: $7-$47 (eliminates price objection, identifies serious prospects)
- Goal: Change their identity from "prospect" to "customer"

### Stage 5: The Main Offer Ascension
- After Stages 1-4, the main offer closes itself
- They already believe in you, see proof, and have paid you
- The sales page just needs to clearly present the offer and remove risk

## FRAMEWORK 3: The Strategic Profits Positioning System

Schefren's biggest insight: **Positioning determines price ceiling.**

**The 3 Positioning Levels:**

| Level | How You're Seen | What They Pay |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| Vendor | "One of many options" | Lowest price |
| Expert | "A trusted specialist" | Market rate |
| Strategic Advisor | "The person who changes my business" | Premium + loyalty |

**Positioning Statement Template (Strategic Advisor Level):**
```
I work with [specific business type] that are generating [revenue range] but
are stuck because [specific bottleneck]. Over [timeframe], I help them [specific
measurable outcome] by [unique mechanism/approach]. Unlike [alternatives],
my approach [key differentiator].
```

**Moving Up the Positioning Ladder:**
- Vendor to Expert: Have a named methodology, publish expertise-proving content, show specific numerical results
- Expert to Strategic Advisor: Speak to business outcomes not deliverables, demonstrate whole-business understanding, charge for strategy not execution

## FRAMEWORK 4: The Continuity Income Blueprint

Schefren's anti-launch system: instead of big launches followed by income droughts, build recurring revenue.

**The 3 Continuity Models:**

**Model 1: The Coaching Continuity**
- Monthly group coaching with recorded calls
- Price: $47-$197/month
- Copy angle: "Stop figuring things out alone - get expert guidance every month"

**Model 2: The Content Continuity**
- Premium newsletter, course vault, or tool library
- Price: $17-$97/month
- Copy angle: "Everything you need to [result] in one place, updated monthly"

**Model 3: The Done-For-You Continuity**
- Monthly service delivery (email management, ad management, content creation)
- Price: $497-$2,997/month
- Copy angle: "Never worry about [task] again - we handle it completely"

**Continuity Sales Page Structure:**
```
HEADLINE: Stop [recurring pain] every single month - here's how to [result]
without [what they hate doing]

PROBLEM: Right now, [audience] spend [X hours/dollars] on [problem] with
inconsistent results. Some months are great. Most are frustrating.

SOLUTION: [Product Name] gives you [specific mechanism] that delivers
[specific result] every month - automatically.

PROOF: [Member name] has been with us [X months] and here's what changed: [result]

THE OFFER: What you get every month...
- [Deliverable 1] - value $XXX
- [Deliverable 2] - value $XXX

JOIN FOR JUST $[price]/month - cancel any time

GUARANTEE: Try it for 30 days. If you don't [specific result],
email us and we'll refund every penny.
```
