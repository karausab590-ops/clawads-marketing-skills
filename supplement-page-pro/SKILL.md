---

## What This Skill Does

Supplement pages fail for one of two reasons: they make claims that get ads banned, or they make no claims at all and bore the buyer into leaving.

This skill produces a **complete supplement product page** that:
- Leads with outcomes (not ingredients)
- Explains the mechanism (why it works) in plain language
- Builds clinical trust without making regulatory-banned claims
- Structures the page to convert skeptical buyers (health product buyers are the most skeptical buyers on the internet)

Compliant with FTC guidelines. Designed for Shopify, ClickFunnels, or any builder.

---

## How To Use

Paste the prompt block below into your AI, fill in your supplement details, and get your full page. Replace all `[BRACKETS]` with your real product information.

## SUPPLEMENT PAGE BUILDER PROMPT

```
Build me a complete, high-converting supplement product page. Use direct response copywriting. 
Every section must be specific to MY supplement — no generic filler.

Use compliant language: avoid "treats," "cures," "diagnoses," or "prevents" [disease name].
Use instead: "supports," "promotes," "helps," "may help," "designed to," "formulated for."

HERE IS MY SUPPLEMENT INFO:
----
Supplement Name: [e.g., AlphaRest PM Sleep Formula]
Form: [Capsule / Powder / Gummy / Liquid / Spray]
Serving: [e.g., 2 capsules before bed]
Primary Health Goal: [e.g., "fall asleep faster, stay asleep longer, wake up refreshed"]
Target Customer: [be specific — e.g., "entrepreneurs and high-performers 30-50 who struggle with racing thoughts at night and wake up groggy"]
Core Problem: [e.g., "can't shut their brain off at night, rely on melatonin that stops working"]
Why Existing Solutions Have Failed Them: [e.g., "melatonin creates dependency, prescription sleep aids leave you foggy, chamomile tea does nothing"]
Key Ingredients (list up to 6): 
  - [Ingredient 1]: [what it does, any clinical backing]
  - [Ingredient 2]: [what it does]
  - [Ingredient 3]: [what it does]
  - [etc.]
Unique Mechanism / What Makes Your Formula Different: [e.g., "dual-phase formula — first phase calms the nervous system, second phase keeps you in deep sleep longer"]
Certifications / Testing: [e.g., GMP certified, third-party tested, NSF, Informed Sport, USDA Organic — or "none yet"]
Country of Manufacture: [e.g., Made in USA]
Price: [e.g., $49.95 / 30-day supply]
Subscription Option: [yes/no, and what discount]
Guarantee: [e.g., 60-day money back]
Any Real Reviews / Social Proof: [e.g., "200 reviews, avg 4.8 stars" or "pre-launch, none yet"]
One Key Differentiator vs Competitors: [e.g., "no melatonin — works without the dependency cycle"]
----

BUILD THIS SUPPLEMENT PAGE:

---

**SECTION 1: SEO TITLE**
Write 1 SEO product title (65 chars max). Lead with the outcome, include the supplement category keyword.

---

**SECTION 2: HERO HEADLINE (3 Options)**
- Option A: Target the frustration: "Still [Problem] Even After Trying [Failed Solution]?"
- Option B: Lead with mechanism: "The [Adjective] [Mechanism Name] Formula That [Outcome] — Without [Feared Side Effect]"
- Option C: Lead with identity: "For [Specific Customer Type] Who [Frustration] — There's Finally a Better Way"

---

**SECTION 3: HERO SUBHEADLINE**
2 sentences. Name the product, state the mechanism in plain language, promise the transformation.

---

**SECTION 4: HOOK / OPEN LOOP (3-4 paragraphs)**
Write the opening story/hook that:
1. Opens IN the customer's lived experience (not "do you struggle with X?" — open mid-scene)
2. Names the specific failure pattern they're stuck in
3. Explains WHY the old solutions failed (mechanism story — this is critical for supplement buyers)
4. Introduces your product as the solution they haven't tried yet

This section should feel like the customer is reading their own diary. Use their exact vocabulary.

---

**SECTION 5: THE MECHANISM STORY**
Write 2 paragraphs explaining WHY your supplement works differently. This is not an ingredient list — it's a story of how the formula was designed to solve the root cause, not just the symptom.

Format:
- Paragraph 1: What's actually causing the problem (the real root cause most people don't know)
- Paragraph 2: How your formula addresses the root cause (name the mechanism)

---

**SECTION 6: INGREDIENT SPOTLIGHT (one block per key ingredient)**
For each key ingredient, write:
**[Ingredient Name]**
> Role: [one sentence — what it does in the body]
> Evidence: [one sentence — clinical backing or traditional use, compliant language]
> Why it's in this formula: [one sentence — how it contributes to the overall mechanism]

---

**SECTION 7: BENEFIT BULLETS (6-8 bullets)**
Format:
✅ **[Specific Outcome]:** [Explanation of how the supplement delivers this — specific, not generic]

Every bullet must describe a real-life change the customer will experience. No ingredient names in bullets — outcomes only.

---

**SECTION 8: WHAT TO EXPECT TIMELINE**
Write a "Results Timeline" block showing what the customer can expect:
- Week 1: [realistic expectation]
- Week 2-3: [building effect]
- Week 4+: [full benefit]
- Long-term: [maintenance/lifestyle benefit]

Use language like "most customers report" or "you may notice" — compliant, not guarantees.

---

**SECTION 9: TRUST ARCHITECTURE**
Write copy for these trust elements:

**Quality & Testing Block:**
Write 3-4 sentences about manufacturing quality, testing, certifications (use what I provided — if none, write aspirational language about what standards were followed).

**The Formula Promise:**
Write 2 sentences about what's NOT in the formula (no fillers, no [common feared ingredient], no [negative thing]). Purity positioning.

**Founder/Brand Story (optional but powerful):**
Write 2 short paragraphs. Why was this supplement created? What personal experience or customer insight drove the formula? Make it real and specific to the health goal.

---

**SECTION 10: SOCIAL PROOF SECTION**
Write 3 testimonial templates covering:
- Testimonial 1: The skeptic ("I've tried everything, was ready to give up on supplements...")
- Testimonial 2: Specific measurable outcome ("After 3 weeks, my [specific metric] improved...")
- Testimonial 3: Life transformation ("I didn't realize how much [problem] was affecting [other areas of life] until...")

Format:
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ "[Quote — 40-60 words, specific, personal, outcome-focused]" 
— [First Name Last Initial], [City or Occupation]
[Verified Buyer badge placeholder]

---

**SECTION 11: HOW TO USE**
Write a clean "How To Use" block:
- Dosage instructions (based on my serving size)
- Best time to take
- What to expect the first time
- Tips to maximize results (hydration, routine, etc.)
- What to do if you don't feel effects immediately

---

**SECTION 12: SUBSCRIPTION VS ONE-TIME BLOCK (if subscription available)**
Write copy for a subscribe-and-save section. Cover:
- Why consistency matters for this type of supplement (science/mechanism reason)
- The financial benefit of subscribing
- Easy cancellation reassurance
- Recommended subscription commitment length ("for best results, most customers use for at least 90 days")

---

**SECTION 13: FAQ (6 Questions)**
Answer these real objections supplement buyers have:
1. Is this safe? Will it interact with my medications?
2. How long until I see results?
3. What if it doesn't work for me? (guarantee)
4. Is this habit-forming / will I become dependent on it?
5. What makes this better than [leading competitor product]?
6. Why is it more expensive than drugstore options?

Answers: Direct, confident, specific. No hedging. Address the real fear behind each question.

---

**SECTION 14: MONEY-BACK GUARANTEE BLOCK**
Write a guarantee block that:
- States the guarantee terms clearly
- Explains the process (easy, no questions asked)
- Flips the risk: frames it as "try it at our expense"
- Ends with a confidence statement about the formula

---

**SECTION 15: CTA + OFFER STACK**
Write the final CTA section with:

**Offer Stack** (list what they're getting):
- The supplement itself (value: $X)
- Free bonus #1 (if applicable)
- Free shipping (if applicable)
- Guarantee
→ **Today's Price: $[price]**

**3 CTA Button Options:**
- Primary (outcome): [e.g., "Start Sleeping Better Tonight →"]
- Value (deal): [e.g., "Get 30 Days Risk-Free for $49"]
- Urgency (if applicable): [e.g., "Claim Your Bottle Before We Sell Out"]

**Risk Reversal Line:**
[e.g., "60-day money back · Free US shipping · Secure checkout · Made in USA"]

---

**SECTION 16: SHORT DESCRIPTION (ads / mobile / listings)**
Write a 3-sentence product description under 75 words. Lead with the customer's pain, present the mechanism, close with the outcome. Suitable for Facebook ads, TikTok captions, or collection page descriptions.

---

After generating all sections, give me:
**COMPLIANCE FLAG:** List any phrases in the copy that might need review for FTC/platform ad policy compliance.
**WHAT TO TEST:** Which headline and CTA to test first, and why.
```

## Pro Tips

- **Pre-launch (no reviews yet)?** The testimonial templates are designed to sound real without being fake — replace them with real ones the moment you have them.
- **Running Facebook/TikTok ads?** Use Section 4 (Hook) as your ad copy. The same mechanism story that sells on-page sells in video.
- **Multiple SKUs?** Run once per product. The ingredient spotlight and timeline vary significantly between products — don't copy-paste across SKUs.
- **Want a landing page version?** Use Sections 4, 5, 6, 10, 14, and 15 in that order for a standalone lander.
