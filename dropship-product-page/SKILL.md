# Dropship Product Page Builder

**Category:** Copywriting / Conversion  
**Works with:** OpenClaw, Claude Code, Cursor, any AI with file context  
**Purpose:** Fill in your product details → get complete, conversion-ready product page copy

---

## What This Skill Does

Most dropship product pages lose sales because they describe the product instead of selling the outcome. This skill takes your raw product info and outputs a **complete, ready-to-paste product page** — headline, hook, bullets, story, social proof, FAQ, and CTA — structured for maximum conversion.

Built on direct response frameworks (Schwartz, Halbert, Kennedy) adapted for Shopify/WooCommerce ecommerce.

---

## How To Use

Paste the following block into your AI chat, fill in the bracketed fields with your real product info, and send. The AI will generate your full product page.

---

## PRODUCT PAGE BUILDER PROMPT

Copy everything below the line and fill in your product details:

---

```
I want you to build a complete, conversion-optimized product page for my dropshipping product using the structure below. 

Use direct response copywriting principles (benefit-focused, objection-handling, outcome-driven). 
Do NOT write generic copy. Every section must be specific to MY product.

HERE IS MY PRODUCT INFO:
----
Product Name: [e.g., ErgoFlow Lumbar Support Cushion]
Category: [e.g., Home Office / Back Pain Relief]
Price Point: [e.g., $39.99]
Target Customer: [describe who buys this — age, situation, pain point. e.g., "office workers 30-55 with lower back pain from sitting all day"]
Core Problem It Solves: [e.g., "chronic lower back pain from sitting at a desk 8+ hours"]
How It Works / Key Mechanism: [e.g., "memory foam contours to your spine's natural curve, distributing pressure evenly"]
Top 3 Features: [list them]
Top 3 Benefits (outcomes the customer gets): [list them]
Biggest Competitor / Alternative: [e.g., "regular office chair cushions, chiropractor visits"]
Why Yours Is Better: [e.g., "ergonomically shaped specifically for lumbar region, not just general seat padding"]
Any Social Proof You Have: [e.g., "500+ reviews, avg 4.7 stars" or "none yet" — I'll create placeholder language]
Price Justification: [e.g., "cheaper than one chiropractor visit"]
Any Guarantee: [e.g., "30-day money back"]
Shipping: [e.g., "free shipping" or "ships in 3-5 days"]
----

NOW BUILD THIS PRODUCT PAGE WITH THESE EXACT SECTIONS:

---

**SECTION 1: SEO PRODUCT TITLE**
Write 1 SEO-optimized product title (60-70 chars). Include: main keyword + primary benefit.

---

**SECTION 2: HERO HEADLINE**
Write 3 headline options using these formulas:
- Option A: [Result] Without [Common Pain/Sacrifice] — [Timeframe]
- Option B: The [Adjective] [Product Type] That [Big Promise] — Even If [Objection]
- Option C: Finally: [Dream Outcome] for [Target Customer]

---

**SECTION 3: HERO SUBHEADLINE**
1-2 sentences. Expand on the headline, name the mechanism, call out the customer.

---

**SECTION 4: HOOK PARAGRAPH (Above the Fold)**
Write a 3-4 sentence hook that:
1. Opens with the customer's exact frustration (use their language)
2. Agitates the problem (what happens if it stays unsolved)
3. Introduces the product as the solution
4. Makes a specific, believable claim

---

**SECTION 5: BENEFIT BULLETS (5-7 bullets)**
Format each as:
✅ [Bold Outcome]: [Specific explanation of how the product delivers this]

Do NOT write feature bullets. Every bullet = a life improvement.

---

**SECTION 6: HOW IT WORKS (3 steps)**
Simple 3-step process. Each step has:
- Step number + short title
- 1 sentence explaining what happens
- Why this step matters to the customer

---

**SECTION 7: THE STORY / WHY THIS PRODUCT EXISTS**
Write 2-3 paragraphs. Tell the origin story or "reason why" for this product. Make it relatable. Connect emotionally to the customer's pain. This is NOT a brand history — it's a narrative that makes the customer feel understood.

---

**SECTION 8: SOCIAL PROOF SECTION**
Write 3 testimonial templates (placeholder format if no real reviews yet) that cover:
- Testimonial 1: Skeptic who was surprised by results
- Testimonial 2: Specific measurable outcome ("I used to X, now I Y")
- Testimonial 3: Recommendation to others in the same situation

Format: 
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ "[Quote]" — [First Name, Location/Occupation]

---

**SECTION 9: PRODUCT DETAILS (Clean format)**
Write a scannable product specs block:
- Materials / What's Included
- Dimensions / Size
- Compatibility / Who It's For
- What makes it different (1-2 differentiators)

---

**SECTION 10: FAQ (5 Questions)**
Write 5 objection-handling FAQs. These must be the REAL questions stopping someone from buying. Answers should be direct, confident, and remove friction.

Questions must address:
1. Does it actually work / will it work for me?
2. How long until I see results?
3. What if it doesn't work? (guarantee)
4. Shipping / delivery time
5. One product-specific objection (you determine what this is based on the product)

---

**SECTION 11: CTA BLOCK**
Write 3 CTA button options (short, urgent, benefit-driven):
- Primary: [e.g., "Get My Pain-Free Back →"]
- Urgency variant: [e.g., "Claim Yours Before Stock Runs Out"]
- Value variant: [e.g., "Start Feeling Better for $39"]

Write 1 risk-reversal line under the CTA button:
[e.g., "30-day money back guarantee · Free shipping · Secure checkout"]

---

**SECTION 12: SHORT DESCRIPTION (For product listings / mobile)**
Write a 3-sentence product description for use on collection pages, ads, or mobile previews. Under 80 words. Lead with the outcome.

---

After generating all sections, give me:
**WHAT TO TEST FIRST:** Tell me which headline option and which CTA to test first, and why.
```

---

## Output You'll Get

A complete product page copy set including:
- SEO title
- 3 headline options
- Subheadline
- Hook paragraph
- 5-7 benefit bullets
- 3-step "how it works"
- Brand/product story
- 3 testimonial templates
- Product specs block
- 5 FAQs
- 3 CTA options + risk reversal line
- Short description for ads/mobile
- Testing recommendation

**Total time to fill in your details: ~5 minutes. Output: paste directly into Shopify, Gempages, or any page builder.**

---

## Pro Tips

- **No reviews yet?** The testimonial templates use placeholder names — replace them as you collect real reviews.
- **Multiple products?** Run the prompt once per product. Build a library of pages fast.
- **Test ads first?** Use Section 12 (short description) as your Facebook/TikTok ad copy starting point.
- **Running a VSL?** Sections 4, 7, and 10 map directly to your script structure.
