## The 12-Part VSL Framework

### Part 1: The HOOK (0ГўВЂВ“30 seconds)
The first thing your viewer sees/hears. Must stop the scroll and create a "I need to watch this" reaction.

**Formula options:**
- **Bold Claim:** "I'm going to show you how [target customer] makes [result] in [timeframe] without [objection]"

- **Story tease:** "Six months ago I was [bad situation]. Today I [amazing result]. And the weird thing that changed everything was..."

**Prompt:**
> Write 5 VSL hook variations for [PRODUCT]. The customer's #1 desire is [DESIRE]. Their biggest fear is [FEAR]. Make each hook under 40 words and force them to keep watching.

### Part 2: CALL OUT the Avatar (30ГўВЂВ“60 seconds)

**Formula:**

**Template:**
> "This is specifically for [TYPE OF PERSON] who [CURRENT SITUATION]. If you've ever [TRIED AND FAILED], if you're tired of [FRUSTRATION], if you know there has to be a better way to [GOAL]... stay with me. What I'm about to share changes everything."

**Template:**

- **Emotional wound:** "I feel like a failure and I'm scared my family will find out I'm struggling"

**Agitation prompts:**

**How to explain the mechanism:**

3. Why it works (the logic)
4. Simple proof it works (the demo or story)

**Template:**
> "The reason [COMMON SOLUTION] doesn't work is because it ignores [HIDDEN FACTOR]. Most people think the problem is [SURFACE ISSUE]. But the real issue is [DEEPER TRUTH]. ## Dropshipping VSL Quick-Start

For dropship products, use this condensed version:

```

Structure:
- Hook (10 sec): Bold claim or curiosity gap
- Problem (15 sec): Agitate the pain
- Solution (20 sec): Introduce the product + mechanism
- Proof (15 sec): Quick social proof or demo result
- Offer (15 sec): Price + bonus + what they get
- CTA (15 sec): Click below, limited time, guarantee

Tone: Conversational, excited but believable. No hype.
```
