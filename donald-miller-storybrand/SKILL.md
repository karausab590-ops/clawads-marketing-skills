# Donald Miller StoryBrand Messaging

Clarify your brand message using Donald Miller's StoryBrand SB7 Framework from *Building a StoryBrand*. Turn confusing messaging into a clear story that makes customers take action.

## How to Use

Tell me about your business — what you sell, who your customer is, and what problem you solve. I'll build your complete StoryBrand messaging guide.

**Just say:** "Build my StoryBrand for [business/product] — we help [customer] who struggles with [problem]"

## What You Get

Your complete SB7 BrandScript:

### The 7-Part StoryBrand Framework

**1. The CHARACTER (Your Customer)**
- Who is your customer?
- What do they want?
*(Your customer is the hero — not your brand)*

**2. The PROBLEM**
- External problem (the practical issue)
- Internal problem (how it makes them feel)
- Philosophical problem (why it's unjust)
*(Villainize the problem, not the customer)*

**3. THE GUIDE (Your Brand)**
- Empathy statement ("We understand how it feels to...")
- Authority statement (why you can help)
*(Brands are Yoda. Customers are Luke Skywalker)*

**4. THE PLAN**
- 3-step simple plan that reduces fear
- Makes the path forward clear and easy

**5. THE CALL TO ACTION**
- Direct CTA (Buy Now, Schedule Call, Start Free Trial)
- Transitional CTA (Download Guide, Watch Demo, Learn More)

**6. FAILURE (What's at stake)**
- What happens if they don't act?
- The cost of inaction

**7. SUCCESS (The transformation)**
- What does their life look like after?
- The hero's transformation

## Output Format

Complete BrandScript with:
- One-liner (elevator pitch using the framework)
- Website hero section copy (H1, subheadline, CTA)
- Email subject line hook
- Social media bio
- Sales call opening script
