# Ecom CRO Audit

**Category:** Conversion / Analytics  
**Works with:** OpenClaw, Claude Code, Cursor, any AI with file context  
**Purpose:** Paste your product page URL or copy → get a scored audit with prioritized fixes and actual rewritten copy

---

## What This Skill Does

Most product pages leak revenue at the same 6 points. This skill runs a structured CRO audit on your existing product page and delivers:

1. **A conversion score (0-100)** with breakdown across 8 dimensions
2. **Top 3 priority fixes** with actual rewritten copy (not just advice — real words you can use)
3. **Headline split test** — your current headline vs. 3 alternatives
4. **CTA optimization** — your current CTA vs. 3 alternatives  
5. **Trust gap analysis** — what's missing that's costing you conversions
6. **Mobile friction audit** — what breaks on mobile
7. **3 A/B test ideas** ranked by expected impact

Built on conversion frameworks used by 7-8 figure ecommerce brands.

## How To Get Best Results

**Give full context:**
- If you have conversion data (current CVR, bounce rate, traffic source), paste it. The audit gets more specific.
- If you know your primary traffic source (Facebook ads vs Google vs TikTok), mention it — buyer intent varies significantly.

**Use it before spending on ads:**
- Run this audit before sending paid traffic to a new product page. Fix the obvious issues first.

**Use it on competitor pages:**
- Works on any public product page URL. Audit your top competitor's page to see what they're doing right (and wrong).

**Scale across SKUs:**
- Run once per product page. If your store has 10 products, 10 separate audits.
