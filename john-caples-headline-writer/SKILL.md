# John Caples Headline Writer

Generate 10 high-converting headlines for any product, landing page, or ad using John Caples' scientifically tested headline formulas from *Tested Advertising Methods*.

## How to Use

Describe your product or service and what it helps people achieve. I'll generate 10 headlines using Caples' proven formulas — tested across thousands of real ads.

**Just say:** "Write headlines for [your product/service/offer]"

## What You Get

10 headlines using Caples' top-performing formulas:

### The Proven Caples Formulas

1. **Self-Interest + Specificity** — "How to [specific outcome] in [timeframe]"
2. **News** — "Announcing the new way to [result]..."
3. **Curiosity** — "What [famous person/expert] never told you about [topic]"
4. **Command** — "[Do this] and [get this result]"
5. **If-Then** — "If you [situation], you can now [result]"
6. **Reason Why** — "[Number] reasons why [product/service] outperforms everything else"
7. **Testimonial Style** — "How [type of person] went from [before] to [after]"
8. **Question** — "Do you [have this problem]?"
9. **The Warning** — "Warning: [common mistake people make]"
10. **The Promise** — "The [fastest/easiest/only] way to [desirable outcome]"

## Caples' Core Rules

- **Lead with the benefit, not the feature**
- **Be specific** — "Lost 17 lbs" beats "Lost weight"
- **Speak to one person**, not the crowd
- **Never be clever at the expense of clarity**
- **Test multiple headlines** — Caples proved small changes = massive differences

## Output Format

For each headline:
- The headline text (ready to use)
- The Caples formula used
- Suggested use (landing page H1, email subject, ad creative, etc.)
