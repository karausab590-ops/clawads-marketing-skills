## What This Skill Does

This skill generates TikTok ad scripts specifically for dropship products. It uses proven TikTok-native formats — POV hooks, "Tell me why" callouts, trending sounds framing — to make your ads feel organic and get watched all the way through.

**Who it's for:**

- UGC creators making content for ecom brands
- Store owners who need scripts they can film themselves in 10 minutes
- Media buyers testing new ad angles

## Why TikTok Ads Are Different (And Why Most Fail)

Most dropshippers copy their Facebook ad formula onto TikTok. That's why they get $0.02 CPM and zero sales.

TikTok is NOT a banner ad platform. It's a **content-first** feed. Users are there to be entertained, not sold to. The algorithm rewards watch time, not ad spend. If your ad feels like an ad, it dies in the first 500 impressions.

The fix: make your ad feel like a creator's organic post. That means:

1. **No logo in the first 3 seconds** — instant trust killer
2. **No price in the hook** — that's salesy, not native
3. **No stock footage** — shaky phone camera > polished studio
4. **Trending language patterns** — "Tell me why I just...", "POV:", "The way that..."
5. **Emotional arc** — problem → shock/agitation → product reveal → relief

When an ad passes the "would I watch this if it wasn't an ad?" test, it converts.

## The 3-Second Hook Formula

You have 3 seconds before someone swipes. Your hook must do ONE of these:

| Hook Type | Mechanism | Example |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **POV Hook** | Makes viewer the protagonist | "POV: you finally fixed your posture" |
| **Curiosity Gap** | Promises a reveal | "I found the product that changed my morning routine" |
| **Pattern Interrupt** | Unexpected visual/sound | Hold product close to camera, speak fast and loud |
| **Tell Me Why** | Invites agreement | "Tell me why I didn't know about this sooner" |
| **Callout Hook** | Targets specific person | "If you work from home and your neck always hurts—" |
| **Relatable Fail** | Show the problem first | "Me trying to blend my protein shake at 6am 😭" |

---

## The UGC Script Structure (Copy This Exactly)

```
[HOOK] — 0-3 seconds
[AGITATE THE PROBLEM] — 3-8 seconds
[PRODUCT REVEAL] — 8-12 seconds
[DEMO / PROOF] — 12-22 seconds
[SOCIAL PROOF LAYER] — 22-27 seconds
[CTA] — 27-30 seconds
```

Total script length: 30 seconds. This is the sweet spot for TikTok conversion ads.

## Pro Tips From High-Converting TikTok Dropship Ads

1. **Film multiple takes, use the messiest/most natural one.** Polished = ad. Raw = content.
2. **Don't show price in the video.** Let curiosity drive them to your bio/link.
3. **Reaction face in thumbnail.** Genuine surprise or "wait what?" expression outperforms text thumbnails.
4. **First comment = social proof.** Post a fake "OMG I got mine last week" comment from your own account right after publishing.
5. **Test 3 hooks for the same product.** Same body, different 3-second hook. One will outperform by 3-5x.
6. **Caption does NOT need to explain.** Short captions work: "I can't believe this worked 😭" outperforms long descriptions.
7. **Re-shoot for the algorithm.** If an organic post about your product goes viral, copy that exact format for your paid ad.

## Notes on Scaling
