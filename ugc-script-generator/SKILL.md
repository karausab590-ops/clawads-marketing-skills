# UGC Ad Script Generator

Write authentic-sounding User Generated Content (UGC) ad scripts for Meta, TikTok, or YouTube ads. Scripts that look real, feel personal, and convert.

## How to Use

Tell me about your product, who it's for, and the main benefit. I'll write a complete UGC script ready to hand to your creator.

**Just say:** "Write a UGC script for [product] — it helps [target customer] [achieve result]"

## What You Get

A complete UGC script including:
- **Hook** (first 2-3 seconds — the scroll stopper)
- **Personal story setup** (the "before" — relatable problem)
- **Discovery moment** ("Then I found...")
- **Product demonstration** (what to show, what to say)
- **Social proof** (results, timeframe, specifics)
- **Call to action** (soft close that doesn't feel salesy)

## The UGC Formula That Works

### Structure (30-60 seconds)
1. **Hook** — Pattern interrupt, bold statement, or relatable frustration (3 sec)
2. **Problem** — "I used to struggle with..." (5-8 sec)
3. **Discovery** — "Until I found/tried..." (3-5 sec)
4. **Proof** — "After X days/weeks, I noticed..." (8-10 sec)
5. **Show** — What to visually demonstrate (10-15 sec)
6. **CTA** — "Link in bio / Try it free / Check it out" (3-5 sec)

### Keys to Authentic UGC
- **First-person, conversational** — "I was skeptical at first..."
- **Specific results** — "Down 8 lbs in 3 weeks" not "lost weight"
- **Include hesitation** — "I almost didn't try it because..."
- **No corporate language** — no "innovative solution" or "game-changing"
- **Pause and imperfect delivery** — sounds more real

## Output Format

Full script with:
- Spoken lines (word-for-word)
- Stage directions in [brackets]
- Estimated timing per section
- Hook variation (2 alternative opening hooks)
