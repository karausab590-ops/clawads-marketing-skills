# Cart Abandonment Email Sequence

Write a 3-email cart abandonment sequence for any ecommerce product. Recover lost sales with emails that remind, overcome objections, and close with urgency.

## How to Use

Tell me about your product, its price, and the main reason people buy it. I'll write all 3 emails ready to load into your ESP (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, etc.).

**Just say:** "Write cart abandonment emails for [product] priced at [price] — people buy it because [main benefit]"

## What You Get

3 complete emails:

### Email 1: The Reminder (Send: 1 hour after abandonment)
- **Subject:** Warm, curious, no pressure
- **Content:** Simple reminder + reinforce the value
- **Tone:** Helpful, not salesy
- **CTA:** Clear button back to cart

### Email 2: The Objection Handler (Send: 24 hours after abandonment)
- **Subject:** Address the #1 reason people don't buy
- **Content:** Overcome the hesitation (price, trust, timing, need)
- **Tone:** Understanding, empathetic
- **Add:** Social proof, guarantee, FAQ answers
- **CTA:** Reassure + easy return to cart

### Email 3: The Last Chance (Send: 48-72 hours after abandonment)
- **Subject:** Urgency-driven (stock, price, offer expiry)
- **Content:** Final push with a reason to act NOW
- **Tone:** Direct, time-sensitive
- **Add:** Scarcity element or small incentive
- **CTA:** Strong, clear, last chance

## Proven Rules for Cart Recovery

- **Don't lead with discount** — train customers to abandon for deals
- **Lead with value**, end with urgency
- **Include the product image** and cart contents
- **Mobile-first** — 60%+ of cart recoveries happen on mobile
- **Personalize** when possible ("Hey [Name], you left something behind")

## Output Format

All 3 emails complete with:
- Subject line + preview text
- Email body (ready to copy-paste)
- Suggested send timing
- Note on what to A/B test
