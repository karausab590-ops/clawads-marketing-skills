# Russell Brunson Funnel Scripts

Write high-converting funnel copy for any stage of your sales funnel using Russell Brunson's proven frameworks from *DotCom Secrets*, *Expert Secrets*, and *Traffic Secrets*.

## How to Use

Tell me what funnel stage you need copy for and your product details. I'll write the complete script or copy block, ready to drop into your funnel.

**Just say:** "Write [funnel stage] copy for [product] — it helps [customer] [achieve result] for [price]"

## Brunson's Core Frameworks Built In

**The Epiphany Bridge** — Tell the story of your "aha moment" that makes people believe
**The Big Domino** — One core belief that, if accepted, makes everything else true
**The Stack** — Present offers so value is obvious and price is justified
**The Perfect Webinar Framework** — 3 secrets structure that builds desire
**The Hook-Story-Offer** — Every piece of funnel copy follows this structure
